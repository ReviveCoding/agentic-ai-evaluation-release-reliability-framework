# Community Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Distinguish evidence, assumptions, and opinions.
- Protect private data, credentials, and vulnerability details.
- Accept feedback and correct mistakes transparently.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, deliberate
misrepresentation, or publication of private security details are not
acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns through the private channel described in
[`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md). Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject
contributions and may restrict participation.
